Profile

Anna Kim is a Korean graphic designer based in Toronto, Canada. She is currently in third year studying Graphic Design in OCADU. She is interested in the intersection of design and craft, and how they can be used to communicate ideas and create experiences. She is also interested in User Experience Design, User interface design, Web development and Typeface design. Outside art and design, she enjoys tools, archives, movie, and music.
