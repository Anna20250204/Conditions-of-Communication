Introduction

What happens when communication no longer feels complete?

This website brings together two projects that examine how information becomes unstable. One project explores overload and interruption as conditions of error. The other explores how meaning shifts across language, translation, and cultural context.

Together, these works ask how systems that promise clarity can also produce confusion, fragmentation, and incompleteness.
